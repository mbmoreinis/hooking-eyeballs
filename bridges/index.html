The City Of Bridges and Much More: Hamburg, Germany

Wow! Have you ever walked across a bridge and look down at the amazing view below? Well, Hamburg, Germany is for you. Hamburg has 2,500+ bridges, a rich history, a great amount of tourist atractions, and also some delicious and amazing food. Hamburg is the place to visit!

How Many Human Beings?

The population of Hamburg is about 1.8 millon inhabitants, which ranks 2nd in Germany behind Berlin. There is more than 5 million people living in the Hamburg Metropolitan Region. For being near the water, that is a lot of people for a city and a Metropolitan Region. This population and the harbor helps with the economics and wealth for the city of Hamburg in a great way.

How Big?

The population in Hamburg is fairly big and the size (land) is too. The size of Hamburg is 292 square miles (755 square Kilometers), which ranks 2nd, just behind Berlin again. In comparison, that is more than 3 times the size of Boston, Massachusetts and just about the same size as New York City! A portion of this size comes from the over 2,500 bridges that are located in Hamburg. That is more bridges than in Amsterdam, Venice, and London combined!

Where is Hamburg?

Hamburg is located in Northern Germany right on the edge of the Elbe River, which leads directly to the North Sea. Because it is right next to the water, it's harbor is a great spot for tourist attractions. It is the country's largest port and commercial center. Due to this, we see a great amounts of trading, exporting and importing of items leading to economic growth. Hamburg is an exceptional coastal city.

Any Good Food?

As expected, the idea for the piece of meat that later became the hamburger was developed it Hamburg. However, the sandwich itself is a total American thing --- stolen from Germany. When visiting Hamburg, you probably wouldn't want a hamburger because of some of the terrific foods that are served there. Due to being on a river and having an ocean nearby, they have some of the freshest fish in Germany. They are also some of the healthy people in the world. Here are some examples of there most flavorful dishes:

  • Fried Plaice (flatfish)
  • Knackwurst (sausage)
  • Rundstück (bread roll)
  • Rote Grütze (Red Berry Pudding)

What to Visit When You're There?

Hamburg is a great place for tourists to visit. Although it is a very big city, it has very good vibes and great attractions for all types of people. Here are some of the best places to visit:

  • Miniatur Wunderland (Museum)
  • Planten un Blomen (State Park)
  • Elbphilharmonie Hamburg (Concert Hall)
  • Hamburg Townhall (City Hall)
  • International Maritime Museum (Maritime Museum)
