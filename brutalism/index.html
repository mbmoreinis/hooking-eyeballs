The amazing impacts of Brutalism on modern day Architecture.

October 3, 2023 by Owen Shea

It is undoubtable that Brutalism has left it's mark on architecture as a whole, though this once prominent architectural style seems to be making a resurgance once again. The new Brutalist movement, better known as neo-brutalism aims to bring back the concrete monoliths that we all know and love. Though neo-brutalism started in the 1950s and slowed down around the late 1980s, it has seen a return in the 2000's. This architectural phenomenon has been met with mixed feelings; critics praise neo-brutalism for it's creativity & bleak architectural design, whilist on lookers call these buildings eyes sores.
