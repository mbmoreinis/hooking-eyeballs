How fishing carelessly negatively impacts the environment and people

October 10, 2023 by Daniel Melnik

The negative impact to the environment thats caused by fishing

One of the negative effects caused to the environment is caused by fishing. When too many fish are taken out of the ocean it creates an imbalance that can erode the food web, eroding the food web leads to a loss of other important marine life, including vulnerable species like sea turtles and corals. the destruction of other life and ecosystems will also loop back around to harm the fish and the industrys that work with fish as well.

Why should you care?

Around one billion people around the world rely on fish as their primary source of protein. As a result, fishing provides an income for millions. Furthermore, seafood is often considered a superfood, packed full of essential nutrients and minerals: Protein enables tissue growth and repair. without fish many people would suffer a loss. considering how people manage fishing maybe the environment can still be restored to a better state.
